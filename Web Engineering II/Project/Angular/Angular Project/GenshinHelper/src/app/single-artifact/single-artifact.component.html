  • Adventurer's Flower

    Flower of Life

    Location:

    Chests

    Description:

    A resilient flower that survived the harsh environment of an ancient city. It is now proudly worn like a medal.

    Lore:

    The ambitious adventurer came across this resilient flower in the depths of a gloomy ruin. Moved by the sight of life sprouting in defiance of a lifeless land, he almost forgot about the treasures buried deep in the ruin. After pausing to ponder it for a while, the adventurer plucked the flower, wore it as a brooch, and ventured forth into the darkness.
